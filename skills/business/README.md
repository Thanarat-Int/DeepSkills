# Business & Go-to-Market skills

Turn a working product into a business.

- **[growth](./growth/SKILL.md)** — Pick the 1–2 channels that fit, design the acquisition funnel, plan the launch, and write the words (landing pages, copy, CTAs) that make people act.
- **[monetize](./monetize/SKILL.md)** — Price on value, package into tiers, and keep unit economics healthy by controlling the cost to serve.
- **[legal-compliance](./legal-compliance/SKILL.md)** — Cover the legal basics (privacy/terms, data protection, licensing) and flag what needs a real lawyer. Practical risk-reduction, not legal advice.
